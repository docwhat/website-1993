Information on MS-Windows

Visiting Microsoft's Web Page is one source of information. They've become rather Win95-centric, so be forwarned.

Also of interest is ftp.cica.indiana.edu's two windows repositories: /pub/pc/win3 and /pub/pc/win95. They are for Windows 3.xx and Windows95 respectively. If you don't have tools for uncompression, you can get them there in the directory /pub/pc/starter.

uiarchive.cso.uiuc.edu is another good site for windows software. Not only do they have a mirror of Cica's /pub/pc/win3 directory (in /pub/systems/pc/cica) but they also have the simtel windows archive mirrored in /pub/systems/pc/simtel/win3. A version of unzip.exe is available there, too (42kb).
